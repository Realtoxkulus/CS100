About the Recipe:

Preparation Time: 15 mins

Cooking Time: 45 mins

Level of Difficulty: Easy

Number of Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 225g unsalted butter
  • 180g caster sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 225g self-raising flour
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 65g caster sugar

Recipe for the Cake:

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180¬∫C.
  2. Beat together the butter and caster sugar until creamy. Add the eggs one at a time, slowly mixing through.
  3. Sift in the self-raising flour, then add the lemon zest and mix until well combined.
  4. Line a loaf tin (8 x 21cm) with greaseproof paper, then pour in the mixture and level the top with a spoon.
  5. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes.

Recipe for the lemon drizzle:

Mix the lemon juice and caster sugar to make the drizzle. Prick the cake all over with a fork, then pour over the drizzle ‚Äì Let the juice sink in. The sugar will form a sweet, crisp topping. Leave the cake in the tin until completely cool before serving.
